Completely crafted out of paper and card, this is a handmade product that can be customized with your photos and messages making it a meaningful gift for your loved one. 

When closed, the box looks like a gift box measuring 4" by 4" by 4" decorated beautifully with a cluster of handmade roses on the lid and a gorgeous bow.

When open, all the sides fall back to reveal a large three-layered card of size 12"x12" filled with 26  spaces for photos or messages in the form of flaps, pull-out message tags and photo spaces. 

Send your photos or messages to codcphotos@gmail.com and we will add them to your box, making it ready for presentation.

We can accommodate up to 28 photos or messages. Ensure the order ID is provided in the email. Photos/ messages will be added randomly throughout the box

 

How to Use 

Lift the lid to open the box and you will find all the sides falling back to reveal a large card with 28 spaces for photos and messages. In each of the 28 spaces provided, we will paste your photos and your personal messages to make it a totally customised gift filled with memories and quotes for your loved one. Photo dimensions will be approximately 3 inches by 3 inches. To close the box, simply fold along the fold lines and place the lid on top, your box is ready for presentation
