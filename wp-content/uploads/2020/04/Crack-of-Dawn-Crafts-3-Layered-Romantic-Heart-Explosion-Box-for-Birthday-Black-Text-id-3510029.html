The ULTIMATE romantic explosion box that can customised with photos and messages making it a meaningful romantic gift for your loved one.

 
When closed, the box looks like a gift box measuring 5 inches by 5 inches by 5 inches decorated beautifully with a striking hot pink romantic pattern and a gorgeous bow.
 
When open, all the sides fall back to reveal a large three-layered card of size 15 inches by 15 inches with more than 75 spaces for photos or messages in the form of flaps, pull-out message tags and clever folds!
 
Included in the pull-out tags are 2 naughty intimate coupons for a private laugh
 
Completely crafted out of paper and card, this is an ideal Birthday gift, anniversary gift, Valentine's Day Gift or other romantic gift for that special someone in your life!

In each of the spaces provided, paste your photos and your personal messages to make it a totally customised gift filled with memories and quotes for your loved one. Photo dimensions should be approximately 3 inches by 3 inches. Once done, fold along the fold lines and place the lid on top, your box is ready for presentation.
